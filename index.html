Davide Spada - CG 2024

Move the camera.

W, A, S, D: Translate the car.

Q, E, Z, C: Rotate the car.

R: Reset the car to its original position.

Mouse moves the camera.

Scroll wheel zooms.

Touch can be used.

Gamepad controls:

  • Left stick: Translate the car.
  • Right stick: Move the camera.
  • Triggers: Zoom in and out.
  • D-pad left/right: Rotate the car left/right.
  • D-pad up/down: Rotate the car upwards/downwards.
  • A or X button: Reset car position and rotation.
